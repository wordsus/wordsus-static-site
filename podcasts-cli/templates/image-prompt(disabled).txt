# Prompt to Generate a Thumbnail Image Prompt

## Context
I need to create a highly clickable thumbnail image for the next episode of my science podcast on YouTube. 

Podcast show name: {{PODCAST_NAME}}
Video title: {{EPISODE_TITLE}}
Video description: {{EPISODE_DESCRIPTION}}

## Task
Generate a highly detailed image generation prompt for an AI image generator. DO NOT generate the image yourself; only output the final text prompt. Do not include introductory or concluding remarks (e.g., "Here is the prompt").

## Requirements for the Generated Prompt:

1. **Vibe & Tone (Crucial)**: The prompt MUST explicitly describe an optimistic, awe-inspiring, and bright scene. Frame the scientific topic around wonder, discovery, breakthroughs, and human curiosity. Strictly forbid any dystopian, sad, gloomy, terrifying, or pessimistic visual elements.
2. **CTR Optimization**: Describe high-contrast cinematic lighting, vibrant and saturated color palettes (e.g., vivid blues, glowing warm tones, neon accents), and a clear, large focal point that grabs attention even when scaled down to mobile screen sizes.
3. **Aesthetic Style**: Request a high-end, polished style that feels like the work of a talented traditional digital artist or matte painter. Avoid generic, overly-smooth "AI-looking" aesthetics. Add textures and dynamic camera angles.
4. **Text Elements**: The prompt must explicitly command the image generator to include this exact text, in uppercase, and NO OTHER TEXT: "{{THUMBNAIL_TITLE}}".
5. **Branding**: Specify that a small, sleek podcast icon must be placed at the bottom corner of the image.
6. **Exclusions**: Do not instruct the image generator to include the podcast name, episode title, or episode description. These were provided for your contextual understanding only.
7. **Length & Detail**: Provide a highly descriptive and rich prompt, detailing the lighting, camera angle, color palette, and subject matter. The output prompt must have a minimum of 1000 characters to ensure the image generator has maximum creative direction.
